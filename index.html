Who We Are

Intellinx established in the year 2012 in India. We are engaged in providing solutions for local and offshore company formation, business development and business consultancy services. We at Intellinx primarily assist offshore clients in setting up and collaborating businesses in India. We help our clients to co-ordinate with financial, legal experts and other relevant business consultants for successful completion of esteemed projects.

Intellinx is professionally associated with multi-disciplined experts spanning the globe. With over 7 years of experience in consulting, our services cover a wide range of options - business development, marketing, and promotion in Brazil, Europe, and the US.

Intellinx have been consulting to companies from a broad range of industries including Construction, Engineering, Agriculture, IT, Food, Local Government.

Intellinx is an Indian consultancy service provider offering a broad spectrum of services to Indian as well as international businesses This makes Intellinx a one-stop solution for all your corporate needs. If you are planning to introduce your product or services in India and abroad, our team can help.

Our goal is optimization - contact us and let us help you reach your goals.
